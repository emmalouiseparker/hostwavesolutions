Refund Policy

At Host Wave Solutions, we strive to provide the best services to our clients. We understand that sometimes circumstances may arise where a refund is necessary. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which we offer refunds for our services. Please read the policy carefully.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

We offer refunds only under the following conditions:

  • Service Cancellations: If you wish to cancel a service before it has been initiated or completed, you may be eligible for a refund. However, any cancellation requests after services have been initiated or completed will not be eligible for a refund.
  • Non-Delivery of Service: If the service was not provided as agreed or has not been delivered within the specified time frame, you may be eligible for a refund. Please contact us within 7 days of the missed delivery date for assistance.
  • Technical Issues: If you experience significant issues with the services provided, such as website downtime or hosting issues, we will work diligently to resolve the issue. If the issue cannot be resolved within a reasonable timeframe, a refund may be provided.

2. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Completed or Initiated Projects: Once a project has been started or completed, we cannot offer a refund.
  • Third-Party Services: If you purchase services from third-party vendors (such as domain registration or hosting services) through us, we are unable to offer refunds for those services.
  • Custom Designs and Development: Customized services, such as custom website designs or development work, are non-refundable once the work has commenced.

3. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at hostwavesolutions@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your name and contact details
  • The service you are requesting a refund for
  • A description of the issue or reason for your request

We will review your request and respond within 7 business days.

4. Refund Processing Time

If your refund request is approved, we will process your refund within 10-15 business days. Refunds will be issued via the original payment method unless otherwise agreed upon. Please note that any transaction fees or bank charges associated with the refund may be deducted.

5. Modifications to the Refund Policy

Host Wave Solutions reserves the right to modify or update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the last update will be indicated at the bottom of the page.
